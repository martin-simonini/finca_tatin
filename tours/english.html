  • Bird Watching

    This area is a real paradise for those who love birds.

    No matter where you are, in the jungle or on the river, you will be able to see birds in their natural habitat.

    Some examples of the birds you may see: Tucans,hawks,hummingbirds,herons,egrets,falcons,owls,
    kingfishers,pelicans,cormorants, etc.

    Bring your Binoculars!.

  • Hikes

    Trails to “Ak’Tenamit”. behind the Finca will take you to the start of the rivier and to Ak’Tenamit (Community development project). Aprox. 20 min.

    Village Tatin(guide required)

    You will walk through the jungle on well marked paths until reaching a cave (Tiger cave) and a water fall(aprox. 30 min), and then you will continue until reaching a indigenous village (Village Tatin) (45 min) and then to Livingston, a Caribbean village home to the Garufunas (aprox. 4-5 hours).

    Return by boat, public or ours

    Night walk through the jungle (minimum of 2 people)

    We will take you with one of our guides through the jungle at night so you can experience the nocturnal life of the animals, and hear the different sounds the animals make.

    Bring your own flashlight.

  • Kayak Tours

    You can explore the the natural beauty that Rio Dulce has to offer by grabbing a kayak and going off on your own. Below we will give you the best places

    Kayak optiones are per hour or all day

    National park Biotopo CHOCÓN MACHACAS.

    A wonderful place, where you can glide through lotus gardens, hearing (and maybe spotting)howlers monkeys, birds, and manatees.

    Options:

    On your own, there and back (Approximately a 4 hour round trip)

    We take you (and your kayak) to the entrance, and you come back on your own (approximately 2 hours)

    Same tour, but with our guide

    The entire tour is on boat, without kayaks.

    Through the Canyon to Livingston

    Birdwatching, lush vegetation, fishermen and open skies.

    Options:

    -In Kayak, on your own (Approximately 3 hours)

    -Go there on kayak, then return on boat, public or ours. You would leave the Kayak in Livingston

    Full moon tour(minimum 2 people)

    If the weather allows it, and specially on full moon nights, we take you through the canyon to enjoy the starry skies, do some animal spotting, have a swim, and, why not, have a nice glass of wine on board
